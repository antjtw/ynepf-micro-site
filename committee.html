Committee

The people who run the division. Scroll each row to meet the team, from our directors to the referees who judge your lifts.

Executive team

The Executive Team sets the direction for the division and leads its day-to-day running.

  • Ant White

    Chair Person

    Leads the division's strategy, governance and links with British Powerlifting and beyond. The safeguarding lead at the top level.

  • Matt Dempsey

    Competition & Operations Director

    Sets up competitions and runs the calendar. Oversees health, safety and safeguarding at events, and works with venues and host clubs.

  • Jo Frost

    Technical & Compliance Director

    Assigns referees, upholds refereeing standards and anti-doping compliance, and runs referee courses for the division.

  • Gemma Gibson

    Finance Director

    Manages the division's accounts, fees, invoices and sponsorship, and oversees merchandise finances.

  • Tarrin Wilson

    Growth & Strategy Director

    Leads media, social media, the website and livestreams, plus merchandise, sponsorship and outreach to juniors and universities.

Supporting committee

The Supporting Committee delivers the work behind our competitions, reporting to the directors.

  • Ed Butcher

    Junior Development Officer

    Builds the junior pathway, links with schools and universities, and supports competitions and coaching for younger lifters.

  • Will Butcher

    Technology Officer

    Improves the tech, systems and tools the division relies on, and runs the competition livestream setup.

  • Ben Gibson

    Health & Safety Officer

    Runs risk assessments, keeps our health and safety policies up to date, and makes sure first aid cover is in place at every event.

  • Matthew Gill

    Equipped Development Officer

    Champions equipped lifting in the division, running workshops and taster sessions and looking after division-owned equipped kit.

  • Karen Horwood

    Records Registrar

    Keeps the divisional records up to date, verifies and certifies new records, and publishes them for everyone to see.

  • Andrew Kearsey

    Equipment Officer

    Keeps a register of all federation equipment, sources and maintains it, and makes sure it meets IPF standards.

  • Sabrina Labuhn

    Entries Coordinator

    Sets up competition entries, handles refunds and changes, plans lifting orders, and reports results to OpenIPF.

  • Gigi Nicolson

    Volunteer Coordinator

    Recruits and assigns volunteers, plans the rotas, and runs the volunteer points system across all competitions.

  • Rachel Steedman

    Equipped Development Officer

    Champions equipped lifting in the division, running workshops and taster sessions and looking after division-owned equipped kit.
