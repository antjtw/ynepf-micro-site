Committee

The people who run the division. The Executive Team sets direction and the Supporting Committee delivers the day-to-day work. Several roles are open to apply for now.

Executive Team

The Executive Team oversees collaboration across the AGMs, providing leadership and direction for the division while engaging in day-to-day operations.

  • Chair Person

    AWAnt White
    • Strategic leadership, governance and external relations (EPA, BP and international bodies)
    • Empowers committee initiatives to develop the division
    • Safeguarding lead at the top level

  • Finance Director

    GGGemma Gibson
    • Manages accounts, audits, fees, invoices and sponsorship revenue
    • Oversees merchandise finances (branding and operations are handled elsewhere)

  • Competitions & Operations Director

    MDMatt Dempsey
    • Sets up competitions and manages the calendar
    • Oversees health, safety and safeguarding at events
    • Works with venue providers and franchise or affiliate hosts

  • Technical & Compliance Director

    JFJoanne Frost
    • Assigns referees for divisional competitions
    • Monitors performance and upholds referee standards
    • Anti-doping compliance
    • Organises referee courses and nominates referees for progression

  • Growth & Engagement Director

    TWTarrin Wilson
    • Media, social media, website and livestreams
    • Merchandise (branding and logistics)
    • Sponsorship and partnerships
    • Junior and university outreach, via subcommittee

Supporting Committee

The non-executive team reports to the directors. Committee members are encouraged to shape their roles and suggest new ideas and improvements alongside their core responsibilities.

  • Finance Officer

    BMBecky Martin

    Reports to the Finance Director.

    • Supports the Finance Director with day-to-day finances
    • Pays expenses and processes invoices
    • Keeps financial records accurate and up to date

  • Safeguarding Officer

    HOHazel O'Neill

    Reports to the Chair and Competitions & Operations Director.

    • Acts as the Designated Safeguarding Lead for the federation
    • Ensures compliance with UK safeguarding standards (DBS checks, safeguarding training)
    • Maintains and updates safeguarding policy, reporting procedures and welfare guidelines
    • Acts as confidential contact point for athletes, volunteers and parents
    • Liaises with external safeguarding authorities if required
    • Provides safeguarding briefings to competition directors and franchise hosts

  • Health & Safety Officer

    BGBen Gibson

    Reports to the Competitions & Operations Director.

    • Conducts risk assessments for competitions and training events
    • Creates and maintains federation-wide health and safety policies (lifting platforms, spotting, equipment checks, venue evacuation)
    • Ensures first aid and medical cover is in place at every event
    • Trains and briefs event organisers and volunteers on emergency procedures
    • Liaises with venue management on compliance with legal requirements

  • Entries Coordinator

    SLSabrina Labuhn

    Reports to the Competitions & Operations Director.

    • Creates new competition items on the Square payment platform
    • Manages competition entries, including issuing refunds and amending entries
    • Plans and circulates lifting orders
    • Records and reports results to the OpenIPF database

  • Volunteer Coordinator

    GNGigi Nicolson

    Reports to the Competitions & Operations Director.

    • Recruits and assigns volunteers for non-franchise competitions
    • Plans and circulates volunteer rotas for non-franchise competitions
    • Manages the volunteer points system and its expenditure across all competitions

  • Equipment Officer

    AKAndy Kearsey

    Reports to the Competitions & Operations Director and Technical & Compliance Director.

    • Maintains an up-to-date register of all federation assets (racks, bars, plates, lights, scales)
    • Sources, purchases or sells equipment in line with federation needs
    • Works with the Technical Director to ensure all equipment meets IPF and technical standards

  • Records Registrar

    KHKaren Horwood

    Reports to the Chair and Competitions & Operations Director.

    • Maintains and regularly updates divisional records across all categories
    • Verifies, certifies and issues new records achieved at sanctioned competitions
    • Publishes updated records promptly on the federation website and socials
    • Liaises with the Technical & Compliance Director to ensure records comply with current rules
    • Maintains a historical archive of records for federation transparency

  • Social Media Coordinator

    Open position

    Reports to the Growth & Engagement Director.

    • Works closely with the committee to draft a social media schedule
    • Manages and maintains social media channels such as Facebook, Instagram and Linktree

    Express interest

    Around 3 hours per month

  • Technology & Systems Officer

    WBWill Butcher

    Reports to the Growth & Engagement Director.

    • Improves the technology, systems and tools the division relies on
    • Runs and develops the competition livestream setup
    • Keeps our digital platforms working smoothly and looks for ways to improve them

  • Equipped Development Officer

    MRMatthew Gill & Rachel Steedman

    Reports to the Chair and Growth & Engagement Director.

    • Acts as the face of equipped lifting within the division
    • Owns, schedules and runs equipped workshops and taster sessions
    • Works with the Equipment Officer to source and maintain division-owned lifting equipment such as shirts, suits and wraps

  • Junior Development Officer

    EBEd Butcher

    Reports to the Growth & Engagement Director.

    • Develops and maintains a junior lifter pathway, from grassroots to elite
    • Builds partnerships with schools, colleges and universities to introduce powerlifting
    • Supports competitions specifically for juniors and university lifters
    • Provides resources and guidance for coaches working with juniors
    • Develops inclusion strategies for under-represented groups
