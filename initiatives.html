2026 division initiatives

Approved by members, enacted by the committee.

  • Competition calendar 2026

    Eight competitions across the division, including three franchise competitions hosted by Parkhead & Wansbeck Powersports, North East Strength Culture and Implexus Gym.

  • Committee restructure

    We have reorganised existing roles and created new ones, with clear expectations and separate responsibilities. Several positions are open to apply for now.

  • Volunteer points system

    A new system lets volunteers earn points that can be used for priority entry, discounts and membership reimbursements.

  • Entry fee changes

    Why our competition entry fees are rising for 2026, and what the money pays for.

  • YNE Worlds 26

    Advancing the divisional championship to serve as a bridge to national competitions, with a clear qualifying total.
