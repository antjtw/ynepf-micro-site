Entry fee changes

As the division grows, we need to raise our entry fees. This brings us in line with other divisions in British Powerlifting and helps us run a cleaner, fairer sport.

Competition entry fees by type, 2025 compared with 2026
Type 2025 2026
Full power£31£40
Bench-only£15£40
Sub-junior£11£40

Why fees are rising

The higher entry fee lets us:

  • continue drug testing, which started at the Nov Open 2025, with the aim of testing at every competition in 2026
  • recognise our volunteers with entry discounts and other rewards for their time and effort

The lifter experience will keep improving over 2026.
