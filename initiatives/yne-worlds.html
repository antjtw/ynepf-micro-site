YNE Worlds 26

Our November competition is a highlight of the calendar, drawing top talent and strong turnouts. In 2026 we are elevating it: YNE Worlds moves to the November Open slot and becomes our divisional championship.

With new restrictions at the Open British Championships, it matters that our lifters have a prestigious platform of their own. To keep the standard high and manage demand, we are introducing a clear qualifying process. This lets the best athletes in the division compete and helps our November championship become our flagship event.

Qualifying process

We analysed all 2025 competition data and set a fair, achievable qualifying threshold for most of the division.

There are two ways to qualify:

  • achieve a minimum of 70 GLP, either at or after the Nov Open 2025
  • win Best Lifter at YNE Worlds 2025

All age classes must meet 70 GLP. Equipped lifters who reach 70 GLP are welcome to enter and place within their category, but they cannot compete for the Best Lifter award.

This competition will not offer bench-only entries.
