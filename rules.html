The rules of powerlifting

Powerlifting has three lifts: the squat, the bench press and the deadlift. These pages explain the rules in plain English, so you know what to do and why a lift might fail.

This is a plain-English guide. It will help you understand the rules. The official IPF Technical Rules are the final word, and referees judge by them.
