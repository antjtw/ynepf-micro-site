Age and weight categories

You compete against people of a similar age and bodyweight. There are age groups and weight classes for both men and women.

This is a plain-English guide. The official IPF Technical Rules are the final word.

Age groups

Your age group is based on your age during the calendar year, not your exact birthday. You can lift up a group if you want to.

Powerlifting age groups and the ages they cover
Age groupAges it covers
Sub-Junior14 to 18
Junior19 to 23
Open19 and over
Master 140 to 49
Master 250 to 59
Master 360 to 69
Master 470 and over

Lifters under 14 can only compete at national competitions.

Weight classes: women

Your weight class is set by your bodyweight at weigh-in. Each class covers everyone up to and including its top weight.
