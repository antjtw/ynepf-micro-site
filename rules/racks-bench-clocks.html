Squat racks, bench and timing

This is a plain-English guide. The official IPF Technical Rules are the final word.

The racks, bench, clocks and scales all meet set standards so competition is consistent.

Squat racks

The racks adjust from at least 1.00 m up to at least 1.70 m, in 2.5 cm steps, and lock in place with pins.

The bench

Bench measurements
PartRequirement
Lengthat least 1.22 m, flat and level
Width29 to 32 cm
Height42 to 45 cm
Upright heightadjustable 75 to 110 cm

Safety stands are always used.

Clocks and scoreboard

Clocks are visible in the venue, on the platform and in the warm-up area, showing the time that has passed. A clear scoreboard shows every lifter by lot number, with the current record on show.

Scales

The scales are electronic and digital, accurate to two decimal places, weigh up to at least 180 kg, and are checked for accuracy every year.
