Referee signals

This is a plain-English guide. The official IPF Technical Rules are the final word.

The chief referee gives the signals that start and end each lift. Miss a signal and the lift fails.

Referee signals for each lift
LiftTo startDuringTo finish
Squatarm down + "Squat"—arm back + "Rack"
Bench pressarm down + "Start"arm up + "Press"arm back + "Rack"
Deadliftno signal—arm down + "Down"

The lights

After each lift, each referee shows a light at the same time: white for a good lift, red for a no lift. Two or more white lights means the lift counts. If the lights fail, referees use white or red flags on the command "Flags". They then raise a coloured card to show the reason for a fail. See what counts as a failed lift.
