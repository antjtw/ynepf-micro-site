Volunteer points system

A points system for volunteers, designed to recognise your time. Scotland and Wales already run this approach, letting volunteers earn priority access and discounts on competitions and memberships.

Earning points

Volunteer points are based on the role and how long you give:

  • Set up or take down: 1 point each
  • Set up and take down: 2 points total
  • Session role (refereeing, platform crew, table team, MC): 1 point
  • Ad hoc volunteering, outside competition: 1 to 3 points. The committee decides based on the size of the task.

Points stack. Volunteer for two sessions and you earn 4 points.

Committee roles

Executive and committee members receive 3 points for each quarter of service after 6 months, with points awarded retroactively from that point.

Banking points

Points can be banked for 24 months. After that, they expire 24 months after being earned.

Spending points

You can spend points in several ways, from early access to competition entries through to a free British Powerlifting membership.

  • Priority entry: 1 point
  • £20 off competition entry: 4 points
  • £20 off, with priority entry: 5 points
  • £40 off competition entry: 8 points
  • £40 off, with priority entry: 9 points
  • Half-price BP membership: 5 points
  • Free BP membership: 10 points

Accessing points

Points are kept up to date on a rewards tracker, available in a public spreadsheet.

  • Priority entry email links are sent to all volunteers 24 hours ahead of general entry
  • Half-price or free competition entry is issued as a discount code on request by email
  • Memberships can be partially or fully refunded on request by email, with proof of purchase
